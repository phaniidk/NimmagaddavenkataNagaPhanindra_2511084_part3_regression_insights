# Final Business Recommendation

## Executive Summary

Regression analysis was conducted to identify the factors influencing Monthly Sales and to provide data-driven business recommendations.

The Multiple Regression Model was selected as the final model because it explains approximately 80.83% of the variation in Monthly Sales.

---

## Key Findings

### Most Important Factors Associated With Monthly Sales

The analysis indicates that the following variables have the strongest association with Monthly Sales:

1. Marketing Spend
2. Inventory Availability Percentage
3. Footfall
4. Customer Rating
5. Regional Factors

These variables collectively explain a large portion of sales variation across stores.

---

## Recommendations For Leadership

### 1. Increase Marketing Investments

Marketing Spend demonstrated a strong positive relationship with Monthly Sales.

Recommendation:
- Increase investment in effective marketing campaigns.
- Monitor return on investment (ROI) regularly.

---

### 2. Improve Inventory Availability

Inventory shortages can directly affect sales performance.

Recommendation:
- Maintain optimal stock levels.
- Improve inventory forecasting and replenishment processes.

---

### 3. Enhance Customer Experience

Customer Rating contributes positively to sales performance.

Recommendation:
- Improve customer service quality.
- Address customer complaints quickly.
- Monitor customer feedback regularly.

---

### 4. Increase Store Footfall

Footfall was positively associated with sales.

Recommendation:
- Run promotional campaigns.
- Improve store visibility.
- Introduce customer engagement programs.

---

### 5. Monitor Regional Performance

Regional differences may influence sales outcomes.

Recommendation:
- Develop region-specific business strategies.
- Allocate resources based on local market conditions.

---

## Variables That Should Not Be Over-Interpreted

Variables with weaker statistical significance should be interpreted carefully because they may not consistently influence Monthly Sales.

Business decisions should focus primarily on variables with stronger statistical support.

---

## Risks And Limitations

The following limitations should be considered:

- Regression analysis identifies association, not causation.
- External factors such as economic conditions, competitor actions, and seasonal events were not included.
- Some variation in Monthly Sales remains unexplained.

---

## Final Recommendation

The organization should prioritize:

1. Marketing effectiveness
2. Inventory management
3. Customer satisfaction
4. Store traffic generation

These areas provide the greatest opportunity for improving Monthly Sales performance.

---

## Conclusion

Based on the regression analysis, the Multiple Regression Model is recommended as the primary decision-support model because it explains over 80% of the variation in Monthly Sales and provides actionable business insights for management.
